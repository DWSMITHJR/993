Rarity & Heritage

Production Rarity

  • Total Production: ~2,800 units (1996-1998)
  • 1997 Model Year: 627 units
  • One of the rarest 911 variants of its era

This 4S is one of the most exclusive air-cooled 911s, prized for its widebody stance and all-wheel drive without the Turbo's complexity.

Key Specs (1997)

  • 3.6L Flat-6 Engine
  • 282 HP @ 6,100 RPM
  • 0-60 mph in 5.5s
  • Top Speed: 168 mph
  • 6-Speed Manual
  • All-Wheel Drive

Why Collectors Love It

  • Turbo-wide body with the large rear wing
  • All-wheel drive for year-round usability
  • Last of the air-cooled era with bulletproof reliability
  • Classic analog feel with modern road manners

History & Documentation

The 1997 Porsche 911 Carrera 4S (Type 993) represents the final evolution of the air-cooled 911. With its wide-body design and all-wheel drive, the C4S combines the best of the 993 generation with the practicality of all-weather capability.
